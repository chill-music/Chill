Helper Information

Your Job

As a Helper, your job is to provide feedback on polls and beta test new ideas/features.

Helpers also give warnings in the chat if necessary and are required to have 2FA on their account.

Permissions / Ranks

  • Access to the Quacky Staff Trello
  • Helper Badge and Hoisted Role
  • Warn Permissions
