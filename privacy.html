Privacy Policy

By using Chill Music, you agree to the following privacy policy.

What information is stored?

Chill Music stores your userID if you've found a bug or something else.

Chill Music also stores your userID if you were in our support server

When a Chill Music suggestion or error you've made get's approved, your userid is stored.

If your guild sets prefix, the guild ID gets stored.

Why we store the information and how we use it.

This information is stored in order to provide you with badges and to notify the bot if you're allowed to run certain commands or not.

The prefix information is used for the bot to know what prefix to respond to.

The profile data is stored in order for us to put custom information on your profile.

Who gets this data?

of course no-one, Don't worry, we're not selling or leaking you data, we love privacy ❤.

Questions and Concerns.

If you are concerned about the data stored Join the Chill Support Server or email us.

How to Remove your data.

If you would like to remove your data, you can email us with the information below:

The information you want removed, your userid, and your guildid (if needed).


Note: We reserve the right to change this without notifying our users.
