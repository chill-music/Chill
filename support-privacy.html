Quacky Support Privacy Policy

By using Quacky Support, you agree to the following privacy policy.

What information is stored?

The following information is stored:

  • When you join the server your join message's messageid is stored as the latest member joined. This gets replaced once someone else joins the server.
  • If you are a staff member and are allowed to apply for Support Team, your userid gets stored until you apply.

Why we store the information and how we use it.

The messageid is stored in case you leave so the bot can edit the join message and say "You left"

The userid to apply for Support Team is stored so the bot knows you're eligible to apply.

If you are a donator and make a custom donator role, your userid and the role's id is stored.

Who gets this data?

The messageid data can only be seen by admins, but really anyone can figure out that you're the latest person to join and get your userid.

The userid to apple for Support Team data can only be seen admins, unless you tell someone you're applying

The roleid and userid is used so you can change the color of the role and for the role to delete when you leave the server.

Questions and Concerns.

If you are concerned about the data stored create a Support Ticket via the !new command.

How to Remove your data.

To remove your data, open a Support Ticket via the !new command.


Note: We reserve the right to change this without notifying our users.
