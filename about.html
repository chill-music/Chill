About Chill Music

What is Chill Music?

Chill Music is a Music bot with lots of utility features. Chill Music has custom features.

Why Choose Chill Music?

A portion of reasons to why you should choose Chill Music:

  1. Chill Quality
    • Have you ever dreamt of quality music without any lag issues or any downtime? Chill Music is what you need.
  2. Filters
    • Chill Music has a large collection of music filters such as 8D, Bass Boost, etc. which can be applied by a single command!
  3. Modern
    • Chill Music uses Discord's up-to-date features to enhance user experience!
  4. Customization
    • Chill Music is greatly customizable, which allows you to setup request channels, prefix and much more!
