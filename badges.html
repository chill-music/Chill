Quacky Badges

What are Badges?

Badges are a virtual emoji that appears on your -user profile or in your -badges profile.

Badges are normally given for helping the bot by voting, partnering, etc.

What are the different types of badges?

On your -user profile, you will see staff, donator, special, and voted badges.

On your -badges profile, you will see the badges on your -user profile (except special) and your remaining badges.

List of Badges

  • Staff Badge - This badge is given to users of the Chill Music Staff Team.
  • Donator Badge - This badge is given to users who Donate to Quacky.
  • Special Badge - This badge is given to certain special users :)
  • Early Supporter Badge - This badge was given to users in the Quacky Support Server on 7/15/2020
  • Bug Hunter Badge - This badge is given to users who find bugs with Quacky as new features get released.
  • Bug Badge - This badge is given to users who find 1 or more bugs with Quacky.
  • Suggestion Badge - This badge is given to users who have made a Quacky Suggestion that has been approved.
  • Partner Badge - This badge is given to users who have Partnered with Quacky.
  • Voted Badge - This badge is given to users who have Voted for Quacky this Month.
