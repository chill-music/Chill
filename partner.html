Partnering with Chill Music Bot

What is Chill Music Bot?

Chill Music is a Moderation and Utility bot to help your Server Moderators. You can Learn More about Chill Music on our About Page.

Parterning a Discord Bot

If you would like to partner your Discord Bot with Chill Music Bot it must meet the following requirements:

  • Your Bot is in at least 125 Real Servers.
  • Your Bot is verified by Discord.
  • Your Bot is not mainly NSFW or has an NSFW Profile Picture/Name.
  • Your Bot does not promote Harrassment, Hate Speech, Violence, or Illegal Activity and Follows the Discord Developer Terms of Service.
  • Your Bot has a Support Server with a Rules Channel and Moderation Team.
  • Your Bot is not a copy of a bot that's already been created.

If your bot meets all these requirements join the Chill Music Support Server and apply with the !partner apply command.

Parterning a Product/Service

If you would like to partner your product/server with Chill Music Bot it must meet the following requirements:

  • The Product has a Support Server.
  • The Product states it is not endorced by Discord (if applicable).
  • The Product must have a Community Discord Server.
  • The Product's Discord Server must have at least 100 Real Human Members.
  • The Product is not a copy of someone else's idea.
  • The Product's website is not a website template/copy of another website.

If your product meets all these requirements join the Chill Music Support Server and apply with the !partner apply command.

Parterning a Discord Server

If you would like to partner your Discord Server with Chill Music Bot it must meet the following requirements:

  • Your Server has at least 150 Real Human Members.
  • Your Server Has and Uses Chill Music Bot.
  • Your Server is not NSFW Related in any way.
  • Your Server does not promote Harrassment, Hate Speech, Violence, or Illegal Activity and Follows the Discord Server Guildelines.
  • Your Server has a Rules Channel and Moderation Team.
  • Preferably, your server is a community server.

If your server meets all these requirements join the Chill Music Support Server and apply with the !partner apply command.
